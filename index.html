Prologue

Since a little kid, I was told that my buried Placenta was my spiritual brother and best friend. One night, he came to me and said that 'Anugrah' means gift and 'You're a gift to the world and a gifted person must do good, must be an avid learner, and compassionate'.

Brother Placenta's wisdom turns out to have effects on later life. During college, I was involved in several campus' organizations. In 2017, I lead a faculty-wide organization and execute several events with my members. This period equipped him with emotional intelligence, empathy, and the adaptability to collaborate with my colleagues.

I also enhanced my learning and writing skills. In college, I started to join research teams and present papers in front of international scholars in Singapore and Surabaya. During my stint as a Community Manager in C4C, I learned and work on many more things, starting from crafting captivating presentations, social media and content planning, project management, and any forms of writing (copy, report, and research).

Currently, I'm looking for a new soil to grow and continue my journey. I fill my days running my online noodle-shop, learning everything I can learn (Excel, Web Dev, Creative Writing), and also reading books.

Latih Logika

As Community manager

A free online critical thinking course

Latih Logika

Is a free online critical thinking course that is supported by UNICEF and The Asia Foundation.

I was responsible in handling the project as a Community Manager .

Basically, my ultimate responsibility is to engage with the audiences and also attract new users through social media interactions. Here are the things I did when I worked there:

  • My early step is to diagnose and analyze if there is any problems that need solving or improve in Latih Logika's Youtube channel and website. To do this, I utilize Youtube analytics and Google Analytics. The output of my analysis is a 20-page report on the traffics of the channel and website. I also propose several steps, strategies, and imrpovement that is presented to UNICEF as our partner.
  • After we have our eye on the problems and solutions, I devised project schedule, project charter, and also content plan complete with audiences persona, language stlye, admin persona, types of content and topics for six months. The tools I utilized are Google Docs, Excel, and Hootsuite.
  • My job also comprises of communicating with vendors, in my case it's media buying and creative agencies. When communicating with creative agencies, I learned to communicate and gave feedback on the designs they made according to our brief. The tricky part is the media buying in which there were lots of consideration regarding the strategies and measurement of digital ads.
  • I was also required to be good at writing, either it is copy, headlines, description, contents, or reports. I wrote the Youtube headlines and description, replying to comments, and also wrote the content planning.
  • I also assisted in my company's training program as assistant which equipped me with presentation skills and also problem solving skills.
